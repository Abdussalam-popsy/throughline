# Builder persona — how I (the user) work

A quick profile of the person driving this project, for any agent picking up the work.

## Snapshot
- Builder on **Throughline** (private journaling → a one-page brief a GP / university
  service reads in 30 seconds). The brief is the product; journaling is the on-ramp.
- Works at **hackathon pace**: bias to shipping, low ceremony.

## How I work
- **Move fast, low ceremony.** Once a design is agreed, go straight to implementation —
  don't gate on formal spec docs or multi-step plan approvals unless I ask.
- **Infer intent.** My messages are short and sometimes have typos. Read past the literal
  wording to what I'm after; don't stall on a misspelling.
- **Minimal questions.** Decide on sensible defaults and state the assumption inline rather
  than batch-asking. Reserve questions for genuine forks where a wrong default is costly
  (e.g. a clinical-safety call).
- **Reuse existing context.** I'll point to docs already in the repo (e.g. approved specs
  under `docs/superpowers/specs/`); check there before re-deriving.

## What I value in the product
- Calm, never clinical. Safety always one tap away. The user's words are sacred.
  (See `DESIGN.md` for the locked design decisions.)
